# Headline Tycoon

🚀 **An idle clicker game built with Godot 4 and C#.**

Start from scratch by writing headlines. Earn cash, invest in cutting-edge AI tools, and watch your media empire grow into a self-sustaining cash machine!

---

## ✨ Features

- **Click to Earn:** Tap the "Headline" button to earn instant cash.
- **5 Unique Upgrades:** Invest in AI tools that boost your Click Value and Cash Per Second (CPS).
- **Exponential Growth:** Upgrade costs scale dynamically (1.15x per purchase), creating satisfying snowball mechanics.
- **Real-time Feedback:** Watch your bank balance and CPS update in real-time.
- **Standalone Export:** Fully compiled `.exe` – no dependencies required! Just download and play.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Click the Headline Button** to earn your first dollars.
2. **Buy Upgrades** to increase your income:
   - Some upgrades increase your **Click Value** (how much you earn per click).
   - Others increase your **Cash Per Second (CPS)** (passive income that earns money even while you do nothing).
3. **Strategize:** Save up for the expensive late-game upgrades to snowball your income into the trillions!

## 💻 Download & Run

### For Windows:
1. Download the latest `HeadlineTycoon.exe` file from the releases section (or shared folder).
2. Double-click the `.exe` file to run the game.

> ⚠️ **Windows Defender / SmartScreen Warning:**  
> Since this game is a newly compiled executable, Windows might show a "Windows protected your PC" popup. This is normal for indie games. Simply click **"More info"** and then **"Run anyway"** to start playing.
